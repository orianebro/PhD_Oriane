- [[Abstract]]
	- The “Virtual grassland” OpenAlea package was developed to provide herbaceous plant models
	  and coupling methods with soil and light transfer modules. It aims at simulating the effects of competition
	  and facilitation interactions at the population and community levels in grasslands.
