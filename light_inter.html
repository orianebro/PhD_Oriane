The \(extinP\) parameter is a daily extinction coefficient and \(parsurrgG\) is a climatic parameter corresponding to the ratio (in radiative energy) of photosynthetically active radiation to the global radiation \(trg(t)\) (around 0.48, Varlet-Grancher et al. (1982)).

\[raint(t)=0.95*parsurrgG*trg(t)*(1âˆ’exp^{âˆ’extinP*(lai(t)+eai(t))})\]

\[extinP = - \frac{1}{lai(t)+eai(t)} ln(1 - \frac{raint(t)}{0.95*parsurrgG*trg(t)})\]

\[extinP = - \frac{1}{lai(t)+eai(t)} ln(1 - ratio\_par\_abs)\]
